Overview

The objective of the project is to redesign the product’s web interface by addressing feedback from the existing consumers to better enhance user engagement.


Target Audience

Teenagers and Adults


THE DESIGN PROCESS


Analyzing the competitors

After experiencing using various competitors' products in the country, the existing flow of newsly lacked in smoother transitions and simpler design for an average consumer


Stakeholder interview

I had a casual chat with the Stakeholders and Product Owners. I quizzed them on short and long-term objectives, vision, and mission, among other things they had planned for the product. My objective was to obtain as much knowledge as possible from them so that I do not become disoriented in the process.

I restrained from talking about technical features, content, search engine optimizations, functionality or anything else that deals with a solution. Rather, the questions we dwelled upon were as follows:

  • What are the brand values ?
  • What sets your company apart ?
  • What are the challenges lying ahead ?
  • What does success look like ?


Analytics Review

I enlisted the assistance of a coworker who works as an SEO Analyst. He sent me online and mobile usage data, as well as crucial information like commonly viewed sites, visitor demographics, bounce rates, and more.


User Interview

I had a chance to chat with a few early users of the product on how their experience was while using it. Following were the crucial questions I discussed with the users.

  • How much time do you spend on performing a task in the product?
  • How do you currently go about a given problem/task?
  • What other similar products have you tried out?
  • What do you like/dislike about the other product?
  • Why would someone use this product?


Identified Problems

  1. Outdated design and Low-quality icons in existing design
  2. Poor rule of thumb.
  3. Crowded navigation section.
  4. Lack of content responsiveness across devices.

Final Scope

  1. Better alignment
  2. Grouping by dropdowns.
  3. User Engagement.
  4. Establish space for displaying ads
  5. Update icons
